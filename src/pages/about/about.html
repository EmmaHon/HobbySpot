HobbySpot application was created to make meeting new people fun and easy. By using our app you can add your own post to meet people with the same interests. You can also search other users posts and comment them if you find activity you would like to participate.

We encourage you to be trustworthy and active when setting up meetings. We want you to became part of our community and invite your friends also

HobbySpot wants to encourage positive and easy interaction between people and we strongly hope that our users act according to these rules.
