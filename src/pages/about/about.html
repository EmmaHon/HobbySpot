Welcome!

HobbySpot is a group project done by 2nd year Software and Mobile development students from Metropolia University of Apploed Sciences. HobbySpot is an app where people can find common interest in hobbies, music, school etc. You can choose categories where you can add interests and other people can view it and add comments. What do you think? Are you willing to find and meet new people who are into same things as you? Find out!
